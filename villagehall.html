Sheet Village Hall information

Sheet Village Hall is managed by the Parish Council and has three seperate rooms for hire as well as modern catering facilities.

Many groups hire the hall for regular activities such as Pilates, Yoga, Keep Fit and Dance classes. These activities will be advertised here, together with contact information in due course.
